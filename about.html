Welcome to My Corner of the Internet

"A wedding is so much more than just a moment caught on camera. It's a story unfolding. It's your story."

More Than a Photographer

Hi, I'm Lou Fasullo, and I'm here to tell you that wedding photography is about so much more than beautiful images. Don't get me wrong — the photos matter, and I pour my heart into making sure they're stunning. But what I really do is help you navigate one of the most important days of your life.

Most couples I work with have never planned a wedding before. You're figuring out timelines, coordinating vendors, managing family dynamics, and trying to stay present in the moment. That's where I come in — not just as your photographer, but as your guide, your advisor, and yes, even your friend for the day.

Guidance & Support

From the moment we connect, I'm here to help. Whether it's advice on creating a realistic timeline, suggestions for the perfect portrait locations, or simply being a calming presence when things get hectic, I've got your back.

Experience-Focused

I recognize that for most couples, this is your first wedding. You need someone who's been through this hundreds of times and knows what works. I bring that experience to ensure your day flows smoothly and beautifully.

Emotional Documentation

My goal isn't just to show you how your wedding looked — it's to help you remember how it felt. The nervous excitement, the tears of joy, the laughter with friends. Those are the moments that matter most.

Multi-Role Approach

On your wedding day, I wear many hats: photographer, timeline keeper, problem solver, and trusted confidant. Whatever you need to make your day perfect, I'm there to help make it happen.

My Approach

When you hire me, you're not just getting someone who shows up with a camera. You're getting a partner who understands the weight and beauty of what's happening. I'll help you plan your timeline so we have enough time for portraits without rushing. I'll suggest the best lighting and locations. I'll coordinate with your other vendors to make sure everything runs smoothly.

And when the day is over and you're looking through your photos, my hope is that you don't just see beautiful images. I want you to be transported back to that moment — to feel the butterflies, to hear the laughter, to remember the way your partner looked at you during your vows.

Because that's what this is really about. Your story. Your love. Your once-in-a-lifetime day.
